Export (MMD)

Click the area to select your save, then use CTRL + C or right-click and then copy.

Download

Import (MMD)

You can import your MyMangaDex data that you exported, paste your save or select your file save and click Import. Options are override.
Merging your data will merge the saved opened chapters, and pick the highest last opened chapter for each manga.

This will erase your current save, export it if you don't want to lose it.

Export (MAL)

Update your MyAnimeList manga list by updating or adding all titles on your MangaDex follow list that have a MyAnimeList id.

You can be extra careful and export your MyAnimeList before using this feature.

Import (MAL)

Update your MyMangaDex last opened chapter with data from MyAnimeList for every title you follow on MangaDex. If saveAllOpened is enabled, a list of chapters is generated.

This functionality doesn't add any manga to your MangaDex follow list, it only update MyMangaDex storage.
This will erase your current MyMangaDex save, export it if you don't want to lose it.

Online Save

Online Save will update your data on a MMD Online Save instance, which you can find here. I propose a default one hosted on my site nikurasu.

You can't currently host your own MMD Online Save for Firefox (working only on Chrome) since permissions don't allow me to make requests to a domain which isn't in the extension manifest, and I can't know what domain you will use.

You only need to set a Username and Password, then when you need to use your data on another computer or another device, log in and your data will be imported.

All data is still saved locally, and the extension only use local storage to retrieve data, you can disable online save and delete all of your saved data at any time.
What Online Save do is updating your save online every time your save is updated locally.
