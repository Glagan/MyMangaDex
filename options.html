Data

Here you can import, export or delete your data.

Export Import Delete

Export

Click the area to select your save, then use CTRL + C or right-click and then copy.

Import (MMD)

You can import your MyMangaDex data that you exported, paste your save and click Import. Options are override.

Import (MAL)

Importing from MAL does the following:

  1. Your list of followed manga on MangaDex is retrieved.
  2. Your MyAnimeList manga list is retrieved.
  3. For every followed manga on MangaDex:
    1. Set the last read chapter as your current chapter on MyAnimeList
    2. Your chapter list is filled with chapters from 1 to your current chapter (if the option is enabled)

Delete

Click the delete button to delete all of your data. A new save will be created with default parameters. Not reversible.

Colors

Colors that exist in HTML are accepted but you can also use a value like #RRGGBB, rgb(r,g,b) or with alpha channel rgba(r,g,b,a).
